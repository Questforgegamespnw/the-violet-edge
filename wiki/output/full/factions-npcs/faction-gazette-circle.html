Gazette Circle

The Gazette Circle is the nervous system of Bellweather's public truth.

It includes the editor, printers, reporters, radio hands, typesetters, newsboys, proofreaders, gossip sources, and citizens who trust the bulletin board more than Town Hall.

Public Role

The Gazette tells Bellweather what happened.

Inside the Violet, that means the Gazette may also help determine what reality believes happened.

Every headline matters.

Private Pressure

The Circle is trapped between truth and consequence.

If it hides too much, rumor fills the gap. If it prints too much, panic becomes public language. If it chooses the wrong word, the Violet may answer that word.

What They Want

  • publish enough truth to preserve trust
  • prevent panic language
  • expose official lies
  • correct rumors quickly
  • preserve a public record
  • protect reporters and printers
  • understand strange radio signals

What They Can Provide

  • public notices
  • rumor correction
  • archives
  • witness statements
  • old clippings
  • printing capacity
  • headline review
  • radio monitoring
  • legitimacy outside Town Hall

What They May Demand

  • access to Council decisions
  • protection from censorship
  • paper priority
  • right to investigate
  • names of the missing
  • public explanation for service bans, curfews, and quarantines

What Can Go Wrong

  • a panic headline becomes doctrine
  • a printed category replaces named people
  • a false survivor is legitimized by publication
  • radio broadcasts using tomorrow's date spread through town
  • the Gazette becomes either propaganda or uncontrolled panic
  • the Violet imitates official notices

Public Interpretation Pressure

The Gazette Circle shifts Public Interpretation faster than almost any faction.

It can stabilize Unknown / Unnamed by refusing premature labels, or it can hard-pivot the town toward Demon / Enemy, Judge / Trial, Utility / Service, or False God / Worship with a single repeated headline.

City Mode Hooks

  • Three possible headlines are on the editor's desk, and all are dangerous.
  • The Committee wants a story killed.
  • A reporter goes missing following a lead in River Ward.
  • The radio receives a broadcast from Bellweather dated tomorrow.
  • A correction notice appears before the error is printed.

Location Ties

Primary location: Gazette Office / Radio Room.

Secondary ties: Town Hall notice board, Market Street, rail depot telegraph room, school bulletin board.

Core Tension

Bellweather needs truth. The Violet hears truth as instruction.
